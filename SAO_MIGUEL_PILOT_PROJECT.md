## üìà **EXPECTED OUTCOMES**

### **Primary Outcomes:**

- **Hypothesis:** Basalt will raise pH from 5.2-5.8 to 6.0-6.5 within 2-3 years

- **Mechanisms:**
  - Improved pH ‚Üí better nutrient availability
